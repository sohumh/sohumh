Compound Words

Starfish isn't the answer, sadly.

Think of two words such that when you place the words back to back, it creates a new word that doesn‚Äôt sound like saying the two words in succession. Note that traditional compound words are pronounced exactly the same as saying the composing words in succession. For instance, starfish sounds like saying star and then fish.
